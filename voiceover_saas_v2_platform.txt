=== OPENING ===
Marketing online usually means juggling a dozen tools — one for keywords, another for your Google rankings, a third for social captions. Digimetrics puts all of it in one place: twenty-seven marketing tools, plus your own Google data, in a single workspace. Let me show you around — this'll take about a minute.

=== PLATFORM TOUR (matches the live 12-beat tour, in order) ===
[beat 1 · Welcome] This is your home screen. Everything's grouped into five areas — S.E.O., Content, A.I. Visibility, Strategy, and your own Google data. You don't have to remember that; the tour walks you through it.

[beat 2 · Find any tool / search] If you know what you want, just search — type "backlinks", "captions", anything. Brand new? Flip on Simple mode and pick a goal instead of a tool, and the app picks the tool for you.

[beat 3 · Browse by category] Or browse by category, to narrow the grid down to just S.E.O., just Content, and so on.

[beat 4 · Every tool is a card] Every tool is a card like this. The badge shows what it costs to run — green means free. A lock means it's part of a higher plan. But here's the nice part: even locked tools give you one real preview run, so you can try before you upgrade.

[beat 5 · Projects] A project is just one website you're working on. Pick it here once, and every tool auto-fills that site's address — and saves your results under it. No retyping.

[beat 6 · Your credits] Most tools run on credits — think of them like tokens. Your plan tops them back up every month, and you can click here any time to see what you've used.

[beat 7 · Connect your Google data] This is a big one. Connect your Google accounts — Search Console, Analytics, and Google Ads — in a click. Once connected, those tools cost zero credits, because it's your own data we're showing you.

[beat 8 · History] Every report you run is saved here. Re-open it later, or re-run it with a single click.

[beat 9 · A.I. assistant] Meet your assistant. Ask it anything — it knows your account and the product, it can explain a result to you, and it can even recommend the right tool and open it for you.

[beat 10 · Ask about anything on screen] And here's my favourite trick. See something on screen you don't understand? Right-click it, and the assistant explains it in plain English — or tells you what to do about it. Only want one number explained? Highlight just that bit first, then right-click.

[beat 11 · Account, billing & support] Your plan, billing, pricing, and support all live up here.

[beat 12 · You're set] That's the whole workspace. To go deeper on any single tool, open it and hit the Tour button next to its name — it walks you through step by step, and ends on a real, live example. Two tools are free to try right now: Caption Generator, and Keyword Analysis. Perfect first runs.
